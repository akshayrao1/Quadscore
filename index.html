NeckWreck

The “Neckwreck” project is a series of events initiated by QUADSCORE, an Independent Event Management Company & Karan Kanchan, a multi-genre music producer from “Gully Gang”. Our vision is to display the Bass Music culture and promote it by bringing in national and global talent to create the right symbiosis between the culture and the audience.
Our goal is to encourage and add to the rise of Bass Music in the otherwise mainstream Indian clubbing culture. It also gives us a platform to showcase emerging sounds and artists that push the boundaries in the industry. NeckWreck, as an event-project, caters to the Bass/Dubstep genre and the Headbanging culture by giving a chance to young artists to make their debut and exposing the Indian audience to fresh new talents. Just like moshing and crowd-surfing, headbanging is a dance move typical to heavy music and for fans like our audience and us. These ‘rail-breaking’ and headbanging events are planned and managed from concept to execution entirely by the team. Having hosted homegrown artists like Gurbax, Sound Avtar, Mosillator, Hamshyre, Resonare and Global artists like Lucchii, ATLiens, Nitti Gritti, Nxsty and more, we try to create the right vibe and an ever-growing community for our fellow bassheads. Our latest feature on a Netflix series called 'Midnight Asia' shows that through this project we want to help strengthen the foundation of the Bass-music culture in India.
